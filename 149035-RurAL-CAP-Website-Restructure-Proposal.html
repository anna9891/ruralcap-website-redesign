Understanding Your Mission

RurAL CAP has been improving the quality of life for low-income Alaskans since 1965. As a statewide nonprofit with a 24-member Board representing every region of Alaska, your organization delivers innovative, community-driven solutions across Education, Housing, and Health & Well-Being programs. Our proposed website restructure will amplify your mission by creating a digital platform that truly serves your diverse stakeholders - from program participants to staff members across the state.

Technical Approach

Our solution addresses all requirements specified in your RFP through a modern, user-centered approach:

Website Restructure Implementation

  • Comprehensive Project Management: Dedicated project manager with weekly progress updates and milestone tracking
  • Site Architecture Optimization: Implementation of your provided sitemap with best practices for navigation and user experience
  • Content Migration & Organization: Professional editing and upload of all provided content with intuitive categorization
  • Dynamic Program Landing Pages: Template-based system allowing easy creation and management of program-specific pages
  • Tiered Permission System: Role-based access control enabling program leads to manage their own content securely
  • Comprehensive Staff Training: Live training sessions with recorded materials for future reference

Key Features Development

Program Pages Include:

  • Rich program descriptions with logo integration
  • Interactive story maps for community engagement
  • Program-specific event calendars with filtering
  • Integrated interest and intake forms
  • Online appointment scheduling system

Resource Library System

Our advanced resource library provides powerful search and filtering capabilities:

  • Filter by program affiliation (Education, Housing, Health & Well-Being)
  • Topic-based categorization for easy discovery
  • Document type filtering (PDFs, forms, guides, videos)
  • Full-text search functionality
  • Access level controls for sensitive materials

Events Management

Program-specific event calendars featuring:

  • Calendar widgets embedded on program pages
  • Category-based filtering
  • Upcoming events ticker
  • Registration integration

Technical Specifications

  • Platform: Modern content management system with headless architecture
  • Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA compliant design ensuring access for all Alaskans
  • Mobile Optimization: Responsive design for rural areas with limited connectivity

  • Branding: Full integration of RurAL CAP visual identity
